YouTube Re-Imagined
I absolutely adore YouTube. It’s the app I use the most right after LinkedIn.
A few months ago, I noticed the left-to-right slider of video categories that appears after you search for something.
I’m not sure if it’s just me, but I feel the slider could be reduced to at most six options. Because even if I’m a fan of an artist, I’m not going to scroll through every single category. Maybe some people do, but to me it creates unnecessary cognitive load.

So, I thought: why not refine these buttons? It might make things easier for users like me.
  • Remove “All”: There’s no real need for it, because after a search YouTube already shows videos, shorts, live streams, etc. all together by default. The “All” option is implied.
  • Videos ---> for long form content
  • Live ---> shown only if relevant live streams are happening
  • Shorts
  • Trending ---> shown only when a song or piece of content from the searched artist is currently trending,
  • Popular Track
  • Recently uploaded
  • Oldest Video
